Analysis and Conclusion

The match chart shows that Yemen has the highest mortality rate of 24.2%, with an average mortality rate of 3.3%....The bubble chart shows that the outbreak of COVID-19 began and peaked in China from January 2020 to February 2020, with very few reports of the outbreak from other countries. While China kept the highest diagnosis rate in March 2020, COVID-19 patients started to emerge in other countries. From April 2020 to June 2020, COVID-19 cases increased rapidly and raged world-widely. The diagnosis rate mounted in the United States for three months, followed by Italy, Spain, France, and Germany in April; Brazil, Russia, United Kingdom, Spain, France, Turkey, and Germany in May; and Brazil, Russia, and India in June. The Sankey chart shows that COVID-19 affected numerous countries with an active rate of 23.5% and a recovered rate of 23.8%. The chart also shows that the United States has the largest proportion in each category (confirmed cases, recovered cases, death toll, active cases).
